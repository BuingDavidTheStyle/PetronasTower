Petronas Tower

The Petronas Tower located in Kuala Lumpur , Malaysia is a iconic skyscrapers due his very tall height of 452 meters (1483 feet), making him the tallest twin towers in the world.

The Construction began in 1993 and Finished in 1998.

He Held The Title of the tallest building from 1996 to 2004 when taipei 101 exceeded him.

César Pelli(1926-2019) Is The One Who Designed The Petronas Tower

The Petronas Towers is a major tourist attraction. Visitors can access the Skybridge and the observation deck on the 86th floor for panoramic views of Kuala Lumpur.
The towers are part of the Kuala Lumpur City Centre (KLCC), a development that includes a park, a shopping mall (Suria KLCC), and a convention center.
